# ELM

This folder contains resources related to the **European Learning Model (ELM)** credential format used for Certificate of Participation in Summer School.

## Contents

- [elm-cps-example](elm-cps-example.json) - Example of an ELM-compliant Certificate of Participation in Summer School.
- [elm-cps-schema](elm-cps-schema.json) - JSON Schema definition of the ELM Certificate of Participation in Summer School.
- [elm-cps-signed](elm-cps-signed.jsonld) - Example of an ELM Certificate of Participation in Summer School signed with JAdES digital signature.
